Membership Benefits

The core of every club ever made in history is it's beliefs, ideals and the most important of all, its members. With a legacy of over 20 years, IEEE UVCE has decided to give away a sentiment of appreciation for all its members.

  • There will be two different tracks, one for 1st years and one for 2nd + 3rd years respectively.
  • Each track will have 4 categories as shown in the points table.
  • All the participants get points based on their activity.
  • Refer the poster for categories of events and the points they carry.
  • Cash prize for a winner of one particular category under each track and special prize for all round performance is up to ₹5,000
